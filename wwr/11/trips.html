Our Featured Rafting Trips

Beginner’s River Adventure

Perfect for first-timers! Enjoy a scenic half-day journey through gentle rapids, led by our certified guides. Learn paddling basics while soaking in stunning views.

Intermediate Rapids Expedition

Take it up a notch! This full-day trip offers a mix of calm sections and thrilling rapids. Ideal for families and groups seeking excitement and teamwork.

Extreme Whitewater Challenge

For the brave and experienced! Tackle powerful rapids and steep drops on this adrenaline-packed adventure. Safety gear and experienced guides included.
